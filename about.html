Welcome to Hacktoberfest

This project is a beginner-friendly space for all those who want to dive into the world of open source. Whether you’re a developer, designer, or just someone curious about contributing — you belong here! Learn, contribute, and collaborate with amazing people from all around the globe during Hacktoberfest.

Contributing to open source helps you grow, build confidence, and be part of something bigger. No contribution is too small — from fixing typos to creating new features, every effort counts.
